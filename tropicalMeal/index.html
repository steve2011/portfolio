Meats like game (bush meat), poultry, pork, beef and fish are all common in African cooking. In rural areas where meat is harder to come by, it is eaten only rarely or on special occasions

Legumes like lentils, beans and peas also form a significant portion of protein in African cuisine. In addition to soups and sauces, legumes are also served mashed or fried in cakes..
